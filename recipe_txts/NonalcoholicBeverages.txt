                     Egg Cream

Recipe By     : 
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Non-Alcoholic Beverages          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        seltzer or club soda -- very
                        cold
       1             "  to 1-1/2" milk
                        tall glass
       1             "  to 1-1/2" U-bet chocolate
                        syrup
       2             "  white 'cream', about

An egg cream is made with very cold seltzer or club soda, U-bet chocolate syrup and ice cold milk. Start by putting about 1 to 1-1/2" milk in a tall glass. Add about the same amount of chocolate syrup.  Add the seltzer and mix vigorously until the ingredients are combine.  If you've done it correctly, there will be about 2" of white 'cream' at the top of the glass.

 An egg cream is never taken through a straw.  You should always drink it from the glass!

                     Homemade Rootbeer

Recipe By     : 
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Non-Alcoholic Beverages          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4         pound  sugar -- granulated
   4 3/4        gallon  lukewarm water
       3         ounce  root beer extract
     1/2      teaspoon  dry bakers yeast
       1           cup  lukewarm water

Put sugar, water, and extract in pot. Mix well. Add yeast to the cup of water. Mix. Add to pot. Blend well. Let settle. Siphon into clean, sterilized bottles. Fill to 1/4-inch of top of bottle. Cap securely. Place bottles on their sides in a warm place for a least five days, longer in cooler weather. Chill and taste.

                     Orange-Cranberry Tea

Recipe By     : Meredith Corporation 1998
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : meg-bev                          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  orange juice
       1           cup  cranberry juice cocktail
       2   tablespoons  sugar
                        cinnamon stick -- 2 inches long
       4                tea bags

In a medium saucepan stir together orange juice, cranberry juice cocktail, sugar, cinnamon, and 2 cups water.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Add tea bags.  Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.

                     Fiesta Hot Chocolate

Recipe By     : 
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Non-Alcoholic Beverages          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  cocoa
       1    tablespoon  flour; unbleached
     1/4           cup  dark brown sugar; packed
       4          cups  milk
       3                cloves; whole
       1                cinnamon stick;broken in 1/2
       2   tablespoons  powdered sugar
   1 1/2     teaspoons  vanilla
                        whipped cream
       4                cinnamon sticks

Mix cocoa and flour in 2-quart saucepan.  Stir in brown sugar, milk, cloves, and 1 stick cinnamon.  Heat just to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly; reduce heat. Pour into 4 cups or mugs.  Serve with whipped cream and cinnamon sticks.
